About

Growing up, writing and music were my creative outlets. In college, I learned photography and found a passion for psychology and social sciences. I was enthralled with human behavior, our relationship to the world around us, and I loved finding ways to explore those connections.

Post-college, I applied that love to helping roles where I honed my customer service skills - actively listening and empathizing in order to root out core problems. Eventually I wanted to weave more creativity into my work, so I took up front end development at Skillcrush, and then a visual UX design apprenticeship at Bloc.

In the field of User Experience, I am at the intersection of my skills and interests at a time when knowledge and consideration of the end user is more important than ever. My favorite thing about UX is what I've enjoyed in all my creative pursuits - working through a process, gathering and analyzing raw information, and turning it into a product or experience that improves people's lives.
